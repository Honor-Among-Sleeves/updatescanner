Welcome to Update Scanner 4

Update Scanner is now a WebExtension.
If you have used Update Scanner before, you can import your previous scan list by following these instructions. Note that this only imports your Update Scanner list, and not the rest of your bookmarks.
Not all Update Scanner features have been ported to this WebExtension yet, thanks for your patience!

Export your Bookmarks

  • Open the Bookmark Manager by clicking the bookmark icon and selecting "Show All Bookmarks".
  • From the "Import and Backup" menu, select "Backup".
  • Save your Bookmarks file somewhere handy.

Import into Update Scanner 4

  • Click the Update Scanner toolbar icon, choose "Menu" then "Restore Pages".
  • Browse to your Bookmarks file.
