Welcome to Update Scanner 4!

Update Scanner has been updated to a WebExtension. If you have used Update Scanner before, you can import your previous scan list by following the following instructions.

Export your Bookmarks

  • Open the Bookmark Manager by clicking the bookmark icon and clicking "Show All Bookmarks".
  • From the "Import and Backup" menu, choose "Backup".
  • Save your bookmark file somewhere handy.
